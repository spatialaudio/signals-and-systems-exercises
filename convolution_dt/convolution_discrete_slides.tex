Calculate the convolution of two finite-length signals / two sequences $x[k]$ and $h[k]$ as
$$y[k] = x[k] \ast h[k]$$
$$y[k] = \sum_{\kappa = -\infty}^{+\infty} x[\kappa] \ast h[-\kappa + k] =
\sum_{\kappa = -\infty}^{+\infty} x[-\kappa+k] \ast h[\kappa]
$$

\begin{pmatrix}

\end{pmatrix}
